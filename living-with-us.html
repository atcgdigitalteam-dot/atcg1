Dementia Care

At Abbey Total Care Group, we offer a secure and supportive environment designed to reduce anxiety and confusion often associated with dementia. Our teams are specially trained in dementia care, providing gentle guidance and support to residents while helping them maintain their skills and independence. We incorporate a range of therapies, activities, and routines to promote cognitive function and emotional well-being. Our approach is centered on understanding each resident's history and preferences, enabling us to provide care that is as unique as they are.

Family Perspective

Keeping families involved and updated on the care of their loved ones is a key aspect of life with Abbey Total Care; we understand how important it is to maintain and strengthen these relationships, which is why we introduced the use of Nourish.

Nourish is a care-centric software which enables clear, regular communication between care teams, medical staff and families - ensuring the highest possible standards of personalised care is maintained at all times.

    Our Nourish care planning software gives full visibility of every aspect of each resident's care, ensuring clear communication between everyone involved in their care.

Food & Dining

Provenance of food

At Abbey Total Care, we prioritise offering a wide-ranging and nutritious selection of meals, understanding the crucial role they play in enhancing our residents' health, well-being, and overall contentment.

Food catered to residents

Mealtimes are a vital aspect of life, and we are committed to catering quality and diverse food. Our catering teams regularly consult with residents to understand their nutritional requirements and food preferences.

Committed to variety

Our menu features an extensive array of options, reflecting the diversity in tastes, cultural backgrounds, and dietary requirements. We aim to ensure residents are well-nourished while catering to their preferences.

Signature dishes

Many of our homes have a signature dish chosen collaboratively by the catering team and residents. Plus, many hold monthly themed food days, providing opportunities for residents to participate in meal preparation.

Our food promise

Our ‘food promise’ is a unique commitment to the quality of catering and the type of food choices that residents enjoy, and is proudly promoted throughout each of our care homes.

Recreation & Activities

Regular Recreation

Dedicated Activities Coordinators in each home ensures there is always something going on! We take a genuine interest in each of our residents, planning activities based on their likes and hobbies. Planned to encourage physical and mental stimulation, all activities are tailored to individual preferences and capabilities, as well as meeting our residents' social, religious and recreational needs. From games and quizzes, to trips further afield and picnics in the park - there's something for everyone at Abbey Total Care homes!

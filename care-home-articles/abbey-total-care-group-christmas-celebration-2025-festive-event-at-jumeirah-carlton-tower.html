Abbey Total Care Group Christmas Celebration 2025

Abbey Total Care Group Ltd, in partnership with Design & Construct (London) Ltd, proudly hosted its annual Christmas Celebration on Saturday, 13 December, at the prestigious Jumeirah Carlton Tower, London. The event brought together colleagues, partners, and guests for an unforgettable evening of reflection, celebration, and festive cheer.

Celebrating People and Purpose

This special occasion highlighted the strength and unity of Abbey Total Care Group, honouring the dedication of our teams who consistently uphold our values and deliver exceptional standards of care. It was a true celebration of the people who make our mission possible.

An Elegant Evening of Entertainment

The evening began with a warm welcome address by Aniket Patel, Chief Operating Officer, followed by inspiring speeches from Jayanti Patel, Managing Director, and Kartik Patel, Chief Commercial Officer, alongside Katie Wright, Regional Director at NatWest Bank.

Guests enjoyed a champagne reception and a beautifully curated dining experience. Entertainment was at the heart of the celebration, with a stunning live vocal performance by Haifa Live, followed by an energetic DJ set from DJ Tazer, keeping the dance floor alive until the close of the evening.

Reflections and Looking Ahead

In his address, Jayanti Patel reflected on the year’s challenges and achievements, expressing heartfelt gratitude for the professionalism and commitment of our teams. He concluded with warm wishes for a joyful Christmas and a prosperous New Year.

The celebration was not only a festive gathering but also a moment to recognise shared successes and look forward with optimism. Abbey Total Care Group remains committed to supporting its people and delivering outstanding care in the year ahead.

Thank you to everyone who attended and contributed to making this event a memorable celebration of unity, dedication, and success.
